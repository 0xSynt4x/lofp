Guidelines and Pricing Information for Construction in Fayd

Base cost to initiate renovation of existing rooms (All prices are in crowns): 
Number  Cost
======= =========
1-10    2000
10+     5000

Base cost to initiate new construction or add additional rooms (includes
renovation of existing rooms):

Number  Cost
======= =========
1-4     5000
5-9     9000
10+     15000

Room costs:

Size    Cost    Description     Capacity (Furnished)
======= ======= =============== =====================
Outdoor 1000    Yard            50 persons
Small   2000    10'x10'         2-3 persons
Medium  2500    15'x15'         5-6 persons
Large   5000    25'x25'         10 persons
Huge    8000    35'x35'+        30+ persons

Room Attributes:

Attribute       Cost    Description
=============== ======= ==================================
Unmarkable      1000    Non-markable, bend-space proof
Garden/Arborium 1000    Mundane flora
Second Floor    500     Room is on a second floor or above
Basement        500     Room is underground
Guild Status    1000

Room Contents:

Item            Cost    Notes
=============== ======= =================================================
Fountain        50
Furnishings     500     Includes any chairs, tables, rugs and tapestries
Portraits       250
Pond            500
Statue          200
Bed             200
Fireplace       200

Portals:

Item            Cost    Notes
=============== ======= ===================================
Door/opening    50      Doors are optionally latchable
Stairway        200
Elevator        3000    Technological device

Individual Security Devices:

Description     Cost    Notes
=============== ======= =============================================
Normal Lock     100     Locks may be placed on both doors and items
Except. Lock    300
Hidden Portal   500     Minimal chance of being revealed by search
Mechanical      2000    Non-harmful, keyed to procedure
Magical         9000    Optionally lethal, infallible, keyed to identity
Animal          2000    Optionally harmful, fallible, keyed to identity
Sec. Personel   5000    Optionally harmful, fallible, keyed to identity
Functional items not listed above are subject to pricing at beginning of construction.

Training areas may optionally teach skills to the highest skill level of any organization member of journeyman or higher status. Knowledge of a skill must be demonstrated at time of renovation. Skills known to members of journeyman of higher status may be taught to any level at a cost of 20,000 crowns per level above 1.

Structures under 10 rooms are guaranteed completion within 30 days of commencement unless otherwise noted at the beginning of construction. Failure to complete construction within alloted time will result in a 50% discount off of total cost.
