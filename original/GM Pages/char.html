Editing a Character

Usage: @EDPLAYER [Player#] [x] [newval?]

   x: 1...First Name       2...Last Name       3...Temp Name
      4...Strength         5...Agility         6...Constitution
      7...Quickness        8...Empathy         9...Willpower
      10..Perception       11..Max Mana        12..Max Psi
      13..Max Body Points  14..Level           15..Weight (lbs)
      16..Height           17..Race            18..Gender
      19..Current Mana     20..Current Psi     21..Current Fatigue
      22..Max Fatigue      23..Total Build     24..Unspent Build
      25..Org Ranking      26..Organization    27..Racial Attribute
Note: Player's Last Name is tied to Truename generation.

Races

  • D - Drakin
  • E - Emphemeral
  • I - Highlander
  • H - Human
  • L - Aelfen
  • M - Mechanoid
  • U - Murg
  • V - Vampire
  • W - Wolfing
  • ! - Shrew [GM only until further notice]
