Foraging/Mining

Foraging

FORAGEDEF [terrain] [item#] [adjective#] [ratio] [val2] [val5]

    This is a definition that states that item# can be found in a room of type [terrain]. The "ratio" variable determines the likelihood of finding this particular item relative to anything else in the area (for example, if one item has a ratio of 10 and another has a ratio of 20, then the second item will be found twice as often as the first). The room terrain for [terrain] is limited to FOREST, MOUNTAIN, PLAIN, SWAMP and JUNGLE. Items should only be forageable if they are prevalent throughout a particular terrain type and if they are generally available throughout all seasons.

Mining

MINDEF [item#] [adjective#] [grade] [copper value] [val2]

    This is a definition that states that item# with adjective# is a mineable item of "grade" (the latter of which may be 'A', 'B', or 'C', in descending value).

ORE

    Ore can be sold or it can be smelted by a character with weaponsmithing skill and then worked into the form of a weapon. PARAMETER2 defines the new item archetype created when the ore is refined. The VAL2 field of an ORE item is transferred to any new item it is worked into. The VAL3 field of an ORE item is its purity, i.e., how much useful metal is contained in the amount of rock. This represents a percentage chance that a smith has of refining any useful metal from the ore. The PARAMETER1 field has multiple meanings. If it equals -1, then no adjective is placed on a new itemconstructed from the material. If it is zero, then the old adjectives of the material are transferred to the adjective to place on a new item created from the material. The PARAMETER3 field specifies the grade of the ore.

MINE_ELEMENT [adj] [val3]

    If a weaponsmith forges a weapon created from a material containing adjective number 'adj' in its first adjective slot then it will set the 'val3' field on the finished weapon as specified. The val5 field is set to a value relative to the smith's skill. The effect is to make it so materials mined from a certain region can be made into weapons with elemental criticals.
