I created INTNUM68 to determine a characters Technological know-how, because I did not want to limit it to simply one's ranking in the Technologists, are linked to a present skill. My hope was that a new skill would be created, Technomancy, and would then incoroporate the rankings listed above. At present, there is no skill and INTNUM68 is controlled by the GM Kreebonker who decides who has learned sufficiently to have their values increased. Technological items around the Realms will use INTNUM68 not see this numbering system, and can only determine their ranking so far through the help of the Technologist's computer, in their guild hall.
