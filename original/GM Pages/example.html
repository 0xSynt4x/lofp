Example 5: The following is a complex example of item scripting. It
           contains a number of  advanced principles that will be
           explained later in  this document.

INUMBER 1617
NAME 32
WEIGHT 4
VOLUME 4
BOW_WEAPON
PARAMETER1 15
PARAMETER2 0
WORN_DON
SUBSTANCE INDESTRUCTABLE
VARIABLE SHOOTER

IFSEEK 180
  IFVERB CONCENTRATE -1
    IFVAR ITEMVAL5 ! TIM
      ADD SHOOTER 1
      IFVAR SHOOTER [ 20
      ECHO PLAYER May your aim be true.
      SPELL 512 30
      ECHO Player Let your enemies not harm you.
      SPELL 133 50
    ENDIF
    IFVAR SHOOTER ] 20
      EQUAL ITEMVAL5 TIM
    ENDIF
  ENDIF
ENDIF

IFVERB LISTEN -1
  ECHO PLAYER For you and only for you I shall reveal the full extent
  ECHO PLAYER of my powers.If you have need of protection and steady
  ECHO player hands you have only to concentrate on me. Should you
  ECHO player need your health restored polish me to a high luster.
  ECHO PLAYER If you are bleeding and in need of staunching the flow
  ECHO PLAYER of blood then sling me onto your back.If you ever need
  ECHO PLAYER light to see by gain my attention by tapping me.
ENDIF

IFVERB WEAR -1
  ECHO PLAYER You sling the bow over your shoulder.
  SPELL 342 50
ENDIF

IFPREVERB GIVE -1
  ECHO PLAYER I am for you and no other.I shall be touched by no
  ECHO PLAYER other hand.
  ECHO OTHERS %n gets a glazed look for a moment and then nods his
  ECHO OTHERS head absently.
  CLEARVERB
ENDIF

IFPREVERB DROP -1
  ECHO PLAYER I shall not leave your hand we are as one now.
  ECHO OTHERS %n looks like he is about to set the bow down and then
  ECHO OTHERS changes his mind.
  CLEARVERB
ENDIF

IFPREVERB PUT -1
  ECHO PLAYER No, I will not be seperated from you.
  ECHO OTHERS %n grasps the bow tightly.
  CLEARVERB
ENDIF

IFITEM -1 WIELDED
  IFVERB LOAD -1
    ECHO ALL As the bow is loaded the arrow shimmers magically.
  ENDIF
  IFVERB RUB -1
    ECHO PLAYER As you polish the bow lovingly, it glows a soft blue
    ECHO PLAYER and speaks to you: Let my power heal thy wounds.
    ECHO OTHERS %n polishes the bow to a high luster, a soft blue
    ECHO OTHERS glow surrounds him.
    EQUAL ROUNDTIME 20
    SPELL 318
  ENDIF
  IFVERB TAP -1
    ECHO OTHERS The bow flares and a brilliant blue light coalesces
    ECHO OTHERS into a floating orb.
    SPELL 113 50
  ENDIF
ENDIF

Example of a CEVENT and IFSEEK

CEVENT 17 30 312
   IFSEEK 110
      IFITEM -1 WIELDED
         RANDOM MUMBOJUM 1 5 0
         IFVAR MUMBOJUM = 1
            ECHO ALL The soulsword exudes a bone chilling aura.
         ENDIF
         IFVAR MUMBOJUM = 2
            ECHO ALL The soulsword flashes with black flames.
         ENDIF
        IFVAR MUMBOJUM = 3
            ECHO ALL The soulsword blasts your mind with thoughts of
            ECHO ALL battle.
        ENDIF
        IFVAR MUMBOJUM = 4
            ECHO ALL The soulsword quivers with excitement at the
            ECHO ALL thought of a battle.
        ENDIF
        IFVAR MUMBOJUM = 5
            ECHO ALL The soulsword groans with a need to taste blood.
        ENDIF
      ENDIF
   ENDIF
