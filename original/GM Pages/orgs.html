Guild Ranking

ORGRANK = build points spent on training within guild

000 < ORGRANK < 50 = Initiate
049 < ORGRANK < 99 = Journeyman
100 < ORGRANK < 199 = Master
200 < ORGRANK = High-Master

When players are training, they may see "You are now rank in the guild."
The rank stated appears to be one tenth that of the ORGRANK variable.
