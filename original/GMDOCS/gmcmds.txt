@activate                    Activates a "monster" that you have @GENMON'ed.
@additem <archnum>           Adds an item into the room you are in.
@announce <MODE> <MESSAGE>   Announce msg to 1:global 2:mindlink
@assist?                     Answers/removes an ASSIST, teleports you there
@close <item name>           Closes an item with no echo.
@delete <item name>          Deletes an item.
@echoplr <job #> <text>      Echoes text to a Cafe patron.
@editem <item name>          Edit item parameters.
@edplayer <job #>            Edits players parameters.
@edn <item record #>         Edits an item by record number (from @pinv).
@eds <Job#> <Skill#> <Level#>Adds a skill.
@entry [text]                Changes your entry echo.
@examine <#>                 Lets you see what types correspond with a number. 
@exclude <job #> <text>      Echoes text to everyone in a room EXCEPT job #.
@exp <job #> <points>        Grant experience to a player.
@exit [text]                 Changes your exit echo.
@find <ARCHNUM>              Finds all of a particular item that is active.
@genmon <Monster #>          Generates a monster in the room with you.
@get <RECORD #>              GETs an item that you've found with @FIND.
@gm                          Puts on your "Host Hat", listing you as a gm.
@go <Room #>                 Lets you teleport to a room.
@goplr <Job #>               Lets you teleport to a player.
@glossary <word>             Let's you look up words for their number.
@grantsp <Job #> <spell>     Gives a spell to a player.
@heal <Job #>                Heals a patron.
@hide                        Removes you from the WHO list.
@invis                       Makes you invisible.
@kill <Job #>                Kills a patron.
@line1 <Job #>               Changes line 1 of a player's description.
@line2 <Job #>               Changes line 2 of a player's description.
@line3 <Job #>               Changes line 3 of a player's description.
@list                        List all the items.
@lock <item name>            Locks an item with no echo.
@look <Record #>             Let's you look inside of a container.
@lwho                        Lists everyone including job and room #'s.
@msg                         Let's you toggle the viewing of Host messages.
@num <Patron Name>           Shows you a Patron's job number.
@oldcomp <script name>       Compiles a script from your home directory.
@open <item name>            Like @CLOSE and @LOCK. It opens an item.
@peek <variable name>        Allows you to view the value of a variable.
@pinv <Job #>                Allows you to view a patron's inventory.
@psi <Job #> <discipline #>  Gives a psi technique to a player.
@queue itemnum counter       Puts something in the monster queue.
@qstat <player #>            Show a "quick" version of the players stats.
@rdata <Room #>              Shows you room data.
@register <player #>         Registers a player for paramutual betting.
@restore <NPC #>             Loads a character from an NPC slot. @Save first!
@rflag                       This is the opposite of @GM.
@rnd <#>                     Generates a random number based on 1 to #.
@save <NPC #>                Saves your persona in an NPC slot.
@sedate <Monster name>       This is the opposite of @ACTIVATE.
@set <variable> <value>      This allows you to set the value of a variable.
@snd <text>                  This echos text into the room you are in.
@spawn <Monster #>           Gen a monster already activated.
@speech <Job #> <text>       This changes the speech pattern of a patron.
@suggest <text>              Adds a suggestion to the suggestion file.
@unhide                      Puts you back on the WHO list.
@unlock                      The opposite of @LOCK.
@unqueue itemnum             Remove an item from the monster queue.
@vis                         The opposite of @INVIS. Makes you visible.
@whisper <Job #> <text>      Lets you whisper to a person not in the room.
@who                         Shows all patrons and monsters and their job #'s.
@yank <Job #>                Yanks a patron into the room with you.
@zap <monster name>          Zaps a monster that is in the room with you.


@set SPELLNUM x              Preps a spell
