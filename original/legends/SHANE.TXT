Dear Shane:

As you know, the temple has been suffering from internal strife
and displeased members. I've spoken to many of them, and certain 
complaints seem to keep cropping up. Not all temple members are
displeased, but a majority of them are, for various reasons, and 
some are extremely unhappy. 

Uneven Attention

Some feel that more attention is given to certain members than 
others in terms of guidance, equipment and events. While this 
can never be eliminated, and is in some cases desirable, I have 
a few suggestions for reducing the perceptions of unfairness:

  * Try to have events focus on different members each time, so
    that each gets attention. 

  * Try to schedule events at different times, so that all may
    participate. 

  * List the assets of the temple in terms of equipment and
    other resources. Describe the criteria by which equipment is
    loaned, sold or traded. There is concern over what equipment
    belongs to the temple, what belongs to you, and where donations 
    end up. 


Chain of Command

Recent departures and arguments in the temple seem to stem in part
from disagreements with you, and the perception that you're
unbending and unable to understand the other's perspective. 
Whatever the reason, perhaps it would be better for the temple if 
ultimate leadership was returned to the High Priestess.

Sincerely,

Taliesin Pendragon, Knight and Field Medic of Amilor
