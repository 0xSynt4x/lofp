Shalana stands only four feet tall, but her psionic power seems to almost set
the air vibrating. Her dark, shining eyes are penetrating in their gaze. Shiny
black hair falls from her heart-shaped face to her tanned shoulders.
She is wearing a dark red desert robe threaded through with tiny gold beads, a
small pale water skin painted with a scene of the desert oasis under the
midnight sky, a thick dark wool cloak patterned with chains of interlocking
chevrons of black and red, some dark red sandals painted with small black
stripes along the straps, a delicate stone anklet of alternating red and black
stones, each painted with a different symbol, a wedding pendant set with a
sparkling fire opal and a thick copper bracelet incised with a spiral pattern.
She is surrouned by a psionic fence.
She is behind a wall of force.
She is surrounded by a force field.
She appears to be healthy.
