My beloved friends: 

As you all know, my wife has been lost to darkness. How Phule's
shadow infected Estara, I cannot say, but many friends including
Myrdhyn and even Queen Zorha have vowed to research the matter.

I have known Phule for many years. Before I joined Amilor, we
were even friends. He is centuries old, and always has reasons
for what he does. I usually disagree with them, but he has them.

Many of you have vowed vengeance on Phule and his allies. I beg
you, hold off. I know some of Phule's wants and weaknesses. Give
me time to speak with him and determine why he did this. He must
have known the consequences. I wish to know what drove him to
this action. Let me reason with him. Surely I can find a way to
have him reverse this tragedy.

Phule may hold Estara's salvation. If that is true, then
aggression against his group may threaten that. If you threaten 
Phule or even kill him, where does that leave Estara? Her
welfare is paramount to me. I'll not tolerate anything that
jeopardizes her return to the light. 

Estara is a fierce fighter, but she is not herself. Please don't 
hold her actions against her. Avoid her at all costs. Despite
her current state, I can't abide by attacks on my wife. I ask
that you stay safe while we work towards her salvation. 

Leave Phule to me. It's a matter of personal honor, and maybe
Estara's only hope. These are trying times for all of us, and
your love and friendship keep me strong. [The parchment is
stained here.] 

Estara, if you read this, please remember the joy and love that
we shared. It is yours for the taking. 

Taliesin Pendragon of Amilor
