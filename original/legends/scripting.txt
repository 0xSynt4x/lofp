Some suggestions for a scripting policy
Dave Goodman (Taliesin) 11/26/97

No unattended scripting. No response within 2 minutes and they get hit.

No littering. Any garments, foraged items or whatever should be picked up or destroyed.

No hoarding. If others are hunting there, the script shouldn't skin,
search and retrieve all treasure.

Disable scripting on request. If another player is trying to hunt and objects to the
scripting, the player should disable it.

No spells that would affect other players (like disjunct or knockdowns).
