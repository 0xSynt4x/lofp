[Taliesin's Toyshop]
Arrayed around you are toys from the four corners of Andor.  A
miniature band plays music for the toy soldiers marching across the
counter, while a small-scale subway train circles you on its track,
whistling and spewing tiny puffs of steam.  There are stuffed animals
representing all the creatures and races of the realms.  Wolfling
puppets can be seen fighting ettin action figures, and underfoot a
small blue drakin repeatedly clashes copper cymbols.  A sign on the
counter lists the toys for sale.
Obvious exits: out.
 
Occasional occurrences:

kneecap! 
A vampire toy flies out from an opening and bites you on the neck!
A stuffed goblin belches in your ear!

[Taliesin's Place]
Like its owner, the room is whimsical but neat.  A bed is provided
for guests who don't levitate during sleep.  Bookshelves hold works
of fiction, philosophy and mental principles, plus mementos of
friends and past exploits.  On the wall is a mirror decorated with a
silver wave, next to the painting of a wolf chasing a scared rabbit
across a meadow in winter.  An incense burner, a candle and a crystal 
orb rest on a low table in the corner for meditation.  Water trickles 
down a sculpture of seashells, tinkling in the manner of a babbling
brook.  Through the window can be seen a simple, well-tended garden. 
Obvious exits: out.
