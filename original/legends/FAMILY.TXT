                               Family Story
	
	
	My great grandma Debbie (who I was named after), is three quarters

Cherokee Indian.  Her dad was Irish and German.  She was born in 1906 in 

Livingston, Kentucky.  She and her twin sister Louan were the youngest of six

children.  She only went to school through the third grade because she

needed to help the family farm.  They grew fruits and vegetables and were very

poor.  One day she was chopping fire wood for the upcoming winter and the ax

flew off the handle and chopped her foot.  She was married at sixteen and had

two children by the name of Margaret and Emma. Emma is one of my grandmothers.
  
	Emma is my mother's mother.  My mother, Connie was married at the age of
	
nineteen.  It seems that all the women in my family marry young, but
