CONSCIENTIOUS p.62      Domain=WORK
  Compares with Enneagram Type One, The Reformer
  Indicators:
    Strong work ethic, "Hard work"
    Strong moral values, "The right thing"
    By the book, "The right way"
    Without error, "Perfectionism"
    Dogged determination, "Perseverance"
    "Order and detail"
    Restrained, cautious, thrifty, "Prudence"
    Pack rat, "Accumulation"
  Compatibility:
    Pairs well with the Dramatic style.
    Also pairs with a moderate Adventurous, Serious or Sensitive style.
    May have solid pairing with Devoted and Self-Sacrificing.
    Pairing with Conscientious styles can work, but prone to power struggles.
    Doesn't pair well with Self-Confident, Aggressive or Vigilant styles.
    Often pairs with Leisurely, although this can lead to frustration.
  Disorder: OBSESSIVE-COMPULSIVE
    Preoccupied with details, rules, lists, order, schedules, etc.
    Perfectionism interferes with task completion.
    Devoted to work to the exclusion of friends and family.
    Inflexible when dealing with morality, ethics or values.
    Unable to discard worn-out or worthless objects.
    Reluctant to delegate tasks or work with those who don't follow same rules.
    Miserly spending style.
    Rigid and stubborn.
    Can't admit to being wrong.
  Disorder Causality:
    May be genetic.
    Most often occurs to first-born children.
    Often caused by over-bearing parents.

SELF-CONFIDENT p.85     Domain=SELF
  Compares with Enneagram Type Three, The Status Seeker
  Indicators:
    High self-regard, "Self-regard"
    Elitist, expects to be treated well, "The red carpet"
    High aspirations, "Ambition"
    Manipulative, "Politics"
    Competitive, "Competition"
    Stature
    Dreams
    Self-awareness
    Poise
  Compatibility:
    Pairs well with Self-Confident style.
    Pairs well with moderate Devoted, Self-Sacrificing, Dramatic styles.
    Clashes with Vigilant, Leisurely, Adventurous and Aggressive styles.
    Poor pairing with Mercurial and Serious.
  Disorder causality:
    Some were spoiled or overindulged as children.
    Some were emotionally or physically neglected or abused as children.
