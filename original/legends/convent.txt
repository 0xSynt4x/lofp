Legends of Future Past
5th Anniversary
Weekend Gathering

On April Fools Day in 1992, a new game called Legends of Future Past ended about 5 months of beta-testing and went online for real.  With daytime costs of $18 and evening costs of $6, this game still had it's share of addicts.  Many moons later, the game still mystifies many.  This weekend has been chosen to allow those who wish to gather to get together and talk about all the good times in the past.

The structure of the weekend is very, very loose.  This is to allow people to mainly do as they please so as to choose how they interact with each other.   Only the two dinners are scheduled, and you really don't have to go to either one of them; there will be a form for you to sign up for them at the end.

Dates: There are rooms for Legends for the following nights.  6/12, 6/13 and 6/14 (Thurs, Fri and Sat) respectively.

Accomodations: We have rooms reserved at the Radisson Inn Marlborough, in Marlborough MA.  For Thurs nite, we have 10 rooms reserved.  The other two nites we have 20.  I can get more if we need it.  These are all at $79/room.  When making your reservations, refer to the group name of 'Legends'.  Check-in time at the hotel is 3:00pm, checkout time is 12:00 noon.  Reservation line is 800 333 3333.

We have 10 rooms for Thursday night, and 20 for each Friday and Saturday night.  20% (2, 4, 4) of the rooms are singles, the rest are doubles.  If we need more, we can almost certainly get them, barring major reservations at the hotel.

Transportation: The hotel is one of the stops of the Marlborough/Westborough shuttle service.  This service runs on 45 minute cycles from Logan Airport and several stops.  The first departure time from the hotel is 4:35am.  The first departure time from Logan is 6:15am.

They also have a pickup service for groups that will probably save you money if you want to coordinate flights with people.  This can be done at either the Logan end or the hotel end and needs to be reserved in advance. The cost for 1 or 2 people is $68.  For each additional person, it's $10.  The vans can hold 11 people.

The phone number for the shuttle is 800 242 0064.

Activities:  The two planned activities are dinner and drinks at the Horseshoe Pub in Hudson MA on Friday evening and a dinner/theatre at Medieval Manor in Boston on Saturday night.

Horseshoe Pub, Friday June 13th, 6:30pm:

A very friendly place that has over 100 craft/microbrewed beers, great atmosphere, good food at better prices and a large following of local Legends people.  Ask your favorite local person for an opinion on the place.

This is pretty close to the hotel, so transportation there will be up to you.   Prices on beer average about $3.00 to $3.25 a bottle for the micros.  Entrees are usually $8.00, which includes potato and vegetable.  Some specials will run as high as $12.  Beef tips are highly recommended.  The entire cost of your evening here at Horseshoe Pub is your responsiblity.

Medieval Manor, Saturday June 14th, 9:30pm..

MOST IMPORTANT NOTE:  Be at Medieval Manor by 9:00pm at the latest.

Medieval Manor is a fun dinner/theatre experience based on a king's court in medieval times.  It is about 2 1/2 hours long and includes dinner, entertainment and beverages.

Dinner starts with loaves of bread, pepper cheese 'trenchers' and bowls of vegetarian soup.  Then, there is spiced shrimp, beef with horseradish sauce and salad.  The final course is roasted herbed chicken and steamed vegetables.

Beverages are dark beer, white wine and sweet apple cider.  These are not potent, but there is a cash bar available before and after the show and during intermissions.

Note: Without quoting their alcohol policy verbatim, they reserve the right to not serve you if you act drunk and refuse/fail a breath test.  You must be 21 years of age.  Also, there are no video cameras allowed and while there is no dress code, your clothes must be intact (sport and/or casualwear is fine, ripped jeans are not).

In order to limit headaches for all involved, a bus will be provided to get there and back.  It will leave the hotel at 8pm and return us there about 1:30am.

Prices:

Dinner at the Medieval Manor is $36 all-inclusive and NON-REFUNDABLE.
The bus ride is $9 and is NON-REFUNDABLE.

PARKING at Medieval Manor is limited in quantity at their small lot and costs $4 per car and is NOT paid in advance.  (This is for those people not taking the bus, of course).
