My friend Sashi (EdH) has taken to standing around BC juggling balls
and doing other tricks.  I think it would be fun if she had some
juggling balls which could do the following actions.  Each suggestion 
has the command, what Sashi would see and what everyone else would
see.  Thanks! 

Sincerely,
Taliesin Pendragon


> i
You are carrying:
    Some brightly colored juggling balls.


> open juggling balls  (OPEN BALL)
    You begin to juggle some brightly colored balls.
    **Sashi begins to juggle some brightly colored balls.


> lean ball
    You lift one leg high and catch a brightly colored ball
    underneath.
    **Sashi lifts one leg high and catches a brightly colored
      ball underneath.


> toast ball
    You juggle two brightly colored balls in one hand.
    **Sashi juggles two brightly colored balls in one hand.


> eat ball
    You catch one brightly colored ball on your chin briefly.
    **Sashi catches one brightly colored ball on her chin
      briefly.

    ("...on the chin..." is okay if it's not easy to make
    the adjective 'his/her/your' match the person EATing the
    juggling balls.)


> kick ball
    You lightly bounce a brightly colored ball off of one foot.
    **Sashi lightly bounces a brightly colored ball off of
      one foot.


> flip ball
    You do a standing backflip.
    **Sashi does a standing backflip.


> close ball
    You throw the brightly colored balls high into the air and catch
    them behind your back. 
    **Sashi throws the brightly colored juggling balls high
      and catches them behind her back.

    ("...behind the back" is okay.)

> punch ball
    You drop your balls and they hit someone in the head!
    **Sashi fumbles the balls and one hits you in the head!
