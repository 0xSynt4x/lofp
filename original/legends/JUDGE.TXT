In one or two brief sentences, please tell me the nature of the dispute.

If all parties agree to binding arbitration, your dispute will be judged by a 
one-person or three-person panel of judges. The charge is 50 gold per judge
from each of the parties. A three-judge panel consists of a warrior, a priest 
and a layman as the letter of the law, the spirit of the law and common
sense. 

This is not a court of law. The panel has no official standing except to 
the extent that you have empowered it. The dispute will be judged based on
whatever laws, ethics and fairness apply at the discretion of the panel.

Each party may choose one representative to speak for them, if desired. The 
panel will question each party until it has determined that enough is known 
to render a decision. No-one is to speak unless spoken to, or risk being 
ruled against summarily. 

Do you wish a three-judge panel? Who do you wish to add? I recommend...
Do any of the judges have any conflict of interest in this case?
Do all parties consent to this arbitration?
The fee will not be returned regardless of the outcome.
May I have the sitting fee, please?
