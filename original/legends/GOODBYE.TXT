My dear friends...
 
I must announce with deep regret that my wife's aunt, the Reija, 
or chieftess, of her tribe, is dying of an incurable disease. This 
means that Shalana must return to the Shazraadi to become their 
new Reija.
 
We always knew this might happen, but never thought it would 
occur so terribly soon. A Reija rules for life or until 
incapacitated. She must renounce all former vows, and marry the 
tribe. I have released Shalana from her vows of marriage, as she 
has released me, and she has returned to the Shazraadi to rule.
 
I love her deeply, and I don't think I shall ever marry another. 
We both pray that she will find time to visit once in a while.
 
May you walk in the light.
 
Taliesin Pendragon, Knight Commander of Amilor
aka Thinra'al Zamfirna
