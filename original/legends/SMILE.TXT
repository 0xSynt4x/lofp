Dear Estara:

After a few days without meeting, it was nice to see your smile again. I take 
it as a good omen that you still wear our wedding ring. 

If you have any ideas on continuing the storyline, please let me know. I
would enjoy hunting with you again, but I will wait for your invitation to do 
so. I am not sure where I may tread and where I may not, so the ball will be
in your court.

I should probably end this message on that note, but I'm an idiot. What
follows is an apology and a proposal, which you may skip reading.

I have been open with you, trusting that you would let me know if I strayed
too far. I'm glad you let me know you felt uneasy, I'm dismayed I caused you
discomfort. We spent many months together, and I miss that easy friendship we 
had. 

When we started this cursed story, I had no idea it would lead to this. I
value you as a friend, and when the plot separated us I was unprepared for
it. Real-life concerns, late-night playing (too many with only 5 or six hours 
sleep) and the course of the story resulted in a strange brew of feelings in
me, which I'm not sure I identified properly. In my quest to return to those
happy days before Phule, I did stupid things. I didn't understand that
Estara's need for time to herself was her player's too. Please forgive my
unperceptiveness. 

I would like for Taliesin and Estara to reconcile. That does not mean that 
their players have to be more than friends, if even that. If Taliesin's 
affections in the game distress you, he need not express them. Whatever 
limits you wish to set I will honor. Whenever you need time to yourself, or I 
stray too far, just tell me. (As bluntly as necessary for me to understand.)

If Shalana's presence makes you uncomfortable, we can devise a means for her 
to leave. Neither Chyme nor I have any problem with that. 

Regardless of what lies ahead, thank you for the time we shared. It was fun.

Tali
