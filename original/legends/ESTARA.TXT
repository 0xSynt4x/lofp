Estara Pendragon

You see Estara Pendragon, a battle-firmed young woman of fierce beauty.  Her
well-muscled body moves with the fluid grace of an experienced fighter. Long
flowing tresses of deep red frame her exquisite and piercing lavender eyes.
She is wearing a beautiful white gold anklet with black and white pearl that
weaves around the star sapphires that adorn it, a white sash with the words
Diplomatic Guest on it, some eldritch titanium mail, a delicate gold wedding
ring with diamonds that twinkle like stars in the night orbiting a star
sapphire, a slender solid gold gorget intricately filigreed and inset with
perfect white pearls, a black silk pouch, a gold hairpin, a gulivira skin
cloak, a black leather satchel, a white silk turban, some deeplander boots,
some gauntlets, some silver earrings encrusted with star sapphires, a heavy
black iron ring shaped like the head of a snarling panther and a thick silver
bracelet painted with a scene depicting the desert sky at midnight.
