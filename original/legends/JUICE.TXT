                               The Juice Bar

The Juice Bar is where you go to get the juice on someone, to pay juice or
even to drink juice.  The Juice Bar is dark.  Everything is black.  The
lights are dim, and fake fog is misted throughout.  There is a bar with high 
stools, a dozen tables, private booths, a dance floor and a small stage.
There is a dressing room behind the stage and a stairway that leads up to
private rooms for those patrons who want to 'lie down' for a bit. The real
service of the Juice Bar is that it's where you go to wheel and deal and be
seen. 

                           The New Coastal Bank

If you're smart, you bank your valuables.  Banks aren't what they used to be 
when there were ten windows and two tellers.  Electronic funds transfer and 
digital dollars put an end to the kind of bank that hadn't changed since 
Billy the Kid was robbing them.  To stay alive in the new century, banks 
became a combination of private storage, financial center and information 
brokerage.
