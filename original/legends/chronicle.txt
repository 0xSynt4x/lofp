              MYSTERY WOMAN IS TALIESIN'S SECRET OTHER WIFE!
                     Taliesin's Hidden Past Revealed!

This reporter has uncovered the stunning events that led to Taliesin
Pendragon keeping two wives. In an exclusive interview for the Chronicle, his 
wife Estara reveals to us what happened. 

"I was shocked!" Estara confides. "He jolts the planet asking for his lover
to come to him and when I show up, there's two of us!" 

Witnesses confirm Taliesin used their energies to project a tremendous call
to his lover. Mass confusion resulted when Shalana, an exotic beauty standing
only four feet high, appeared out of nowhere to answer his call. 

Shalana herself was dazed on learning that the husband she thought dead for
eight long, lonely years had been living with another woman and using an
assumed name. 

"My Thinra!" she sobbed. "The man I knew as Thinra'al Zamfirna, whom I once
saved from death, looked at me as if I was a stranger! He knew not his own
name!" 

Using her psionic abilities, the distraught Shalana probed Taliesin and
learned that the memories of his life before Fayd had been locked. Once she
released them Taliesin was able to piece together what happened. 

He revealed that over 8 years ago, Shalana found a man wandering lost in
the desert, sunburnt, dehydrated, half-blind and near death. She took him
back to her tribe and healed him. Shalana named the man Thinra'al Zamfirna,
meaning Madman of the Wastes. They were able to bring his body back to
health, but his mind had been shattered. They taught him their tongue and
trained him in their ways. They fell in love.
