INTRODUCTION

AI Cards are an attempt to simulate human personality using a collection of
models of human behavior.  The system below is a minimal prototype only.
Qualities can have a minimum, maximum, typical and current value.  These are
denoted by the following symbols:

  xxx <=  Minimum. Must be greater than or equal to the xxx minimum level.
  <= xxx  Maximum. Must be less or equal to the xxx maximum level.
  = xxx   Current. The quality is currently at the xxx level.
    xxx   Typical. The quality when unmodified is at the xxx typical level.
 cur/typ  The current and typical values.

This may also be shown as:  min <= typ <= max = cur


TYPES OF CARDS

BODY cards represent the physical condition of the body.  The card reflects
its ability and current state.  Body qualities include:
  Characteristics:
   Hair color
   Hair length
   Hair style
   Eye color
   Weight
   Height
   Build
   Age

  Movement:
   STAM  Stamina          Maximum energy
   RECP  Recuperation     Energy regeneration rate
   STRN  Strength         Power of movement
   AGIL  Agility          Speed of movement
   DEXT  Dexterity        Accuracy of movement
  An action exerts STRN force and reduces STAM by the same amount.  Each 
  period the STAM is increased by the RECP amount.  As stamina drops, 
  strength, dexterity and agility can be reduced.  When the stamina reaches
  zero or below, the subject is stunned.  Agility determines how quickly
  actions can be performed.  Dexterity determines the success of the action.

  Health:
   CONS  Constitution      Ability to withstand physical damage
   VITL  Vitality          A combination of vital signs
   RESP  Respiration       The rate of breathing
   PRES  Blood pressure    The blood pressure reading
   PULS  Pulse rate        The current pulse rate
   DAMG  Damage            The amount of damage to all the vital organs
   BVOL  Volume of blood   The amount of blood left
   TOXC  Toxicity level    The level of poison in the blood
   OXYG  Oxygen            The level of oxygen in the blood
