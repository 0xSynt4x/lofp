The room fills with the presence of Amilor...
You hear Amilor boom, "Stand my children for I bring news most joyous."
You hear Amilor boom, "Long have you stood vigilant, now your efforts are
rewarded."
You hear Amilor boom, "The foul one Dahkahn is no more."
You hear Amilor boom, "The gods of light reign triumphant."
You hear Amilor boom, "Yes indeed go forth and spread the good news..."
You hear Amilor boom, "But..still do not fail in your tasks for always there
is the chance that he or another could return." 
You hear Amilor boom, "He has been imprisoned in such a way that it is
unlikely he will return." 
You hear Amilor boom, "I will say no more on the manner of his prison lest
those who would aid him hear."
You hear Amilor boom, "But because you have ever fought his presence, I
thought to reward you with this news." 
As the presence of Amilor fades away, you feel blessed.
