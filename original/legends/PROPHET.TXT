Prophet:
 
I thank you for the concern that you and all Way members show 
for the welfare of my wife, Estara. Your motives are noble, and 
I'm glad that our quibbles over whose claim to protect and 
avenge her came first did not result in significant strife 
during her possession by darkness. 
 
It saddens me that once again our common desire, that of 
protecting Estara, comes between us. Immanuel has been quite 
vocal in opposing my decision to keep both of the wives to whom 
I made solemn vows. While his inquiring into my personal life 
and his quite vocal opinions have been irritating, even 
insulting, I have tried to keep the discussion merely that.
 
However, I must respond when he attempts to use mindlink to 
contact Shalana's father. He has not the power to reach across 
the planet to where my father-in-law lives, but if the Hodja 
starts traveling to find his daughter, it's possible that he'll 
come close enough for Immanuel to reach him.
  
I love my father-in-law, but it is he who blocked my memories 
and sent me away when I married Shalana. If he were to find 
Shalana and myself, I fear that he would take us from Fayd or 
block our memories. Estara needs me now, and nothing will take 
me from her side, not the Way, not the Hodja, not even the 
temple of Amilor.
 
I consider Immanuel's attempt to reach the Hodja an act of 
betrayal against Estara and my entire family. I have warned him 
to cease, and if he continues I will use whatever force I
require to stop him. I ask that you counsel him to stop meddling 
in my family's affairs, and that you not lead the Way against me
should I be forced to act against Immanuel in self-defense. 
 
Thank you for your consideration.
 
Taliesin Pendragon nee Thinra'al Zamfirna
