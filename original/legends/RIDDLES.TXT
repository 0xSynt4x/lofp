THE HUNT
   If you want to win a prize,
   Hear the clues and use your eyes.
   Come to the fountain right away,
   And listen to the things I say.

THE PRIZE
   The prize will not make you a king,
   But it's worth more than a ring.
   It could boost your strength or health itself,
   But the game's the thing and not the wealth.

THE RULES
   The first to find it wins it all,
   Find it then give me a call.
   You have five minutes to make do,
   If still no luck you'll get Clue #2.

THE MOUSEHOLE - Clue #1
   You know it by what isn't there,
   Its owner may give girls a scare.
   Find it to prove you're not dumb,
   You'll know it by the little crumb.

THE CREAMERY - Clue #2
   It's called the best part of the crop,
   You'll find it laying on the top.
   Go to where it's kept in store,
   And there you'll find the prize galore.

THE COUNTER - Clue #3
   You put things on it and inside,
   It's not that tall but pretty wide.
   You look right through it, and within,
   But look behind it and you'll win!
