                              Taliesin's Vows

After years of extensive research as a master healer, I have proven
conclusively that if your parents didn't have children, neither will you. 

What I have also found is that every race, since before the first pages of
history, task their males and females to bond together and honor the
blessings of life by producing offspring. We teach our children the past,
protect and raise them in the present and then spend our lives ensuring their
future. 

In Estara I have found a kindred spirit, one who daily displays her passion
for life's joys and responsibilities. She is the soulmate I have sought for
lo these many years to share the journey to the future. Never have I met one
for whom I've felt so deeply. We hunt together, play together and rejoice 
together. She senses my feelings as if they were hers. I know her thoughts as 
they form in her. We are but one spirit in two bodies. I pledge now, before
Amilor and all assembled, to dedicate my life to Estara and our family to
come. 

Estara Starr, I love you as I love life. You stir in me feelings so profound
that I cannot profess them. Your beauty and strength, so pleasing to the eye, 
wells from within as testament to your inner quality. I will do all in my
power to protect you and provide you a home where we can grow as individuals
and a family. There is no limit to my love for you. The more that I give, the 
more you fill me with love, and I will cherish you for as long as I draw
breath. I vow it.
